Questions? We Have Answers.

How long does it take to deliver? expand_more
Our standard delivery time is 24-48 hours once we receive all the content (photos/text) from you. For express orders, we can deliver within 12 hours.
Is the link private? expand_more
Yes, absolutely! We provide you with a unique, unlisted URL. We can also add password protection to your gift if requested.
Who has access to the photos and text I share? expand_more
Only our dedicated design team has access to the content you upload. We follow strict internal privacy standards to ensure your photos, letters, and memories are kept 100% confidential.
Can my gift website be searched on Google? expand_more
No. We build all our surprise websites with instructions that tell search engine crawlers (like Google) not to index the site. It will remain completely hidden from search results and can only be viewed by people you share the link with.
Can I request to delete my website or photos later? expand_more
Yes! Your comfort is our priority. If you wish to take down the website or delete your photos and text from our storage at any time after delivery, just send us a message on WhatsApp and we will delete everything permanently within 24 hours.
